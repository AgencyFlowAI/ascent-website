Roadmap

JUN 2026

PF-1 "GRACE"

First flight. Hybrid motor, ~1 km apogee, full avionics.

AUG 2026

PF-2

Scaled engine, higher altitude, improved recovery.

NOV 2026

PF-3

Mid-altitude push. New materials, bigger motor.

FEB 2027

PF-4

Higher impulse engine. Pushing past 5 km.

MAY 2027

PF-5

First vehicle potentially requiring CAA large rocket permission.

AUG 2027

PF-6

10+ km altitude. New propulsion systems and materials.

NOV 2027

PF-7

Pushing into the upper atmosphere. Advanced recovery systems.

FEB 2028

PF-8

20+ km. Testing at altitude where the air starts thinning out.

MAY 2028

PF-9

30+ km. Final proving flights before the Photon programme.

AUG 2028

PF-10

~40 km. Last Pathfinder. The full flight record for a CAA spaceflight licence.

2028-29

Photon-1

100 km. The Karman line. Everything the programme has been building toward.

THE PROGRAMME

Three Phases to Space

30 vehicles across three generations. Each phase pushes higher, faster, and harder than the last.

01
PHASE 01

Pathfinder

10 VEHICLES / 1-40 KM

The proving ground. Ten flights testing propulsion, recovery, and avionics before committing to a space-capable vehicle.

02
PHASE 02

Photon

10 VEHICLES / 40-100+ KM

The mission vehicles. Photon-1 targets the Karman line. This is where the programme either works or doesn't.

03
PHASE 03

Horizon

10 VEHICLES / BEYOND

What comes after space. Pushing the limits of what one person can build and fly.
