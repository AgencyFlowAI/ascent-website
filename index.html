Roadmap

JUN 2026

PF-1 "GRACE"

First flight. Hybrid motor, ~1 km apogee, full avionics.

JUL 2026

PF-2

Scaled hybrid, ~40 mm chamber, 3 km apogee.

AUG 2026

PF-3

Larger hybrid, ~50 mm chamber, 5 km apogee.

AUG 2026

PF-4

Final hybrid, ~60 mm chamber, 8 km apogee. Last flight before liquid engines.

OCT 2026

PF-5

First liquid engine. Ethanol/N2O, ~200 N, 12 km apogee.

DEC 2026

PF-6

Scaled liquid, ~300 N, 18 km apogee.

FEB 2027

PF-7

~500 N liquid engine - Photon-1 S2 prototype. 25 km apogee.

APR 2027

PF-8

~600 N liquid engine, 32 km apogee.

MAY 2027

PF-9

~800 N liquid engine, 40 km apogee. Mach 2 territory.

JUN 2027

PF-10

~1000 N engine or first two-stage test. 50 km apogee. Dress rehearsal for Photon-1.

JUL 2027

Photon-1

100 km. The Karman line. Everything the programme has been building toward.

THE PROGRAMME

Three Phases to Space

30 vehicles across three generations. Each phase pushes higher, faster, and harder than the last.

01
PHASE 01

Pathfinder

10 VEHICLES / 1-50 KM

The proving ground. Ten flights testing propulsion, recovery, and avionics before committing to a space-capable vehicle.

02
PHASE 02

Photon

10 VEHICLES / 40-100+ KM

The mission vehicles. Photon-1 targets the Karman line. This is where the programme either works or doesn't.

03
PHASE 03

Horizon

10 VEHICLES / BEYOND

What comes after space. Pushing the limits of what one person can build and fly.
